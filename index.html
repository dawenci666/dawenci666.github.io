Hello!!

I'm a recent B.Sc.(Research)-Mathematics graduate from Shiv Nadar (Institution of Eminence deemed to be University).

On the mathematical side, broadly speaking I'm interested in Analysis. I have completed several advanced Analysis courses, including Topology and Functional Analysis. I have expanded my knowledge of Basic Operator Theory and Spectral Theory, and my undergraduate thesis was based on these topics.Specifically, my thesis explored the problem of Classifying Essentially Normal Operators on a complex separable Hilbert space under the guidance of Dr. Amber Habib. You can find a report on my thesis here.

In addition to mathematics, I am interested in theoretical computer science. I have taken multiple courses in this field and also enjoy studying it independently. I am particularly fascinated by Randomized Algorithms and am always eager to learn more about them.
